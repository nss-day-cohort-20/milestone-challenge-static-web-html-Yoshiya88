About me:

My name is Joshua Meyers, I am an aspiring web developer and student at Nashville Software School. Outside of programming I am an English as a second language tutor specializing in teaching Japanese students. I have been working with Japanese for nearly 5 years, and spent some time living in Saitama, Japan. In my free time I enjoy playing video-games, discovering music, bodybuilding, swimming, rockclimbing, and collecting mechanical keyboards.
